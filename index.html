Rules

Residents of Greenbrier Hills: Please observe all rules stated at the pool and abide by rules provided in your HOA Manual. Any use of the pool outisde of the stated rules is strictly forbidden and action will be taken against those who choose not to observe the requirements. You are directly liable for any broken rules including but not limited to the lack of supervision of children, which includes anyone under the age of 18 in your care.

Any child including a minor under the age of 18 years old is not allowed to use the pool or be on pool property without the direct consent and supervision of an adult present at the location of the pool. Any child or minor below the age of 18 years old located on the pool premise without direct resident adult supervision will be required to leave the premise immedately or authorities will be asked to provide assistance.
